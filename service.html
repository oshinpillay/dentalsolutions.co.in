SERVICES

Complete dental check-up

Our complete dental checkup services include thorough examinations, professional cleanings, X-rays, and personalized care to ensure optimal oral health. We identify issues early, providing preventative treatment and tailored solutions for lasting smiles.

Digital X-ray

Digital X-ray is an advanced imaging technique that uses digital sensors to capture images of the inside of the body, offering faster results, lower radiation exposure, and enhanced image quality compared to traditional methods.

Teeth Scaling and polishing

Teeth scaling and polishing are dental procedures that remove plaque, tartar, and stains from teeth. Scaling cleans below the gumline, while polishing smooths the surface, promoting better oral hygiene and preventing gum disease.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation

Full mouth rehabilitation is a comprehensive dental treatment plan that restores or replaces all the teeth in both the upper and lower jaws. It addresses issues like tooth decay, misalignment, and jaw function, combining procedures such as crowns, implants, bridges, and more to restore overall oral health and appearance.

Dental implants

Dental implants are titanium/zirconiums posts surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing teeth. They act as artificial tooth roots, providing a stable foundation for crowns, bridges, or dentures, offering a permanent and natural-looking solution.

Removable Partial Dentures

Removable partial dentures are prosthetic devices used to replace one or more missing teeth while preserving remaining natural teeth. They are custom-made to fit comfortably, are removable, and help restore chewing, speaking, and smile appearance.

Braces/Orthodontics

It involve attaching brackets and wires to teeth to gradually straighten and align them. Regular adjustments by the orthodontist ensure proper movement. Once aligned, the braces are removed, and a retainer is often provided to maintain the new position.

Complete dentures

Complete dentures are removable prosthetic devices used to replace all missing teeth in either the upper or lower jaw. They restore function, improve appearance, and enhance the ability to speak and chew for individuals with no remaining natural teeth.

Crown and Bridges

Crowns and bridges are dental restorations used to restore damaged or missing teeth. A crown covers a damaged tooth, while a bridge replaces one or more missing teeth by attaching to adjacent healthy teeth, restoring function and appearance.

Rotary Single sitting RCT

Rotary single sitting Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a modern, time-efficient procedure used to treat infected or damaged teeth. It involves using rotary instruments for faster cleaning and shaping of the root canals, completed in one visit to relieve pain and preserve the tooth.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Wisdom tooth extraction is a surgical procedure to remove one or more third molars (wisdom teeth) from the back of the mouth. It is often performed when these teeth cause pain, infection, or alignment issues, or if there isn't enough space in the mouth.

Extraction

Tooth extraction refers to the removal of a tooth from its socket in the bone. Anterior tooth extraction involves removing teeth at the front of the mouth (incisors and canines), typically due to decay, trauma, or overcrowding. Posterior tooth extraction involves the removal of back teeth (premolars and molars), often due to severe decay, infection, or impaction.

Maxillofacial Prosthesis

Maxillofacial prosthesis is a specialized dental prosthesis used to restore or replace facial structures lost due to trauma, congenital defects, or disease. It includes prostheses for the eyes, nose, ears, fingers(including hands and legs) or entire facial regions, helping to improve appearance and function.

Dental Veneers

Dental veneers are thin, custom-made shells placed over the front surface of teeth to improve appearance. They correct issues like discoloration, chippings, or misalignment, offering a natural-looking, durable solution for a brighter smile.

Dental Fillings

Dental fillings are used to restore teeth damaged by cavities or decay. The decayed portion of the tooth is removed and filled with materials like amalgam, composite resin, or porcelain, ensuring function and appearance are restored.

Teeth Whitening Procedure

Teeth whitening involves applying a bleaching agent to remove stains and discoloration. The procedure is typically done in-office by a dentist or using custom-fit trays at home. Results can be seen almost immediately, leaving your smile brighter and more radiant.

Invisalign/Aligners

Invisalign involves custom-made clear aligners that gradually shift your teeth into place. You'll wear each set for about 1-2 weeks and have periodic check-ups with your orthodontist. Once treatment is complete, a retainer is often provided to maintain your new smile..
